Features making everyday use a breeze

Intuitive interface. At a glance calendar view. Convenient notifications.
Everything you need for your Toastmasters activities.

Discover

Tampines Changkat? Smedley Chapter One? Roastmasters? F.R.O.G. Club? Search for and visit any club in the world.

Meetings

Big and clear Calendar view of club meetings. See one, multiple or all clubs meetings in a District.

Attend

Attend a meeting in just one click. When you arrive, the app intelligently asks you to confirm. Now, who wants to miss any meetings?

Social

See who else is attending. It's more fun if more club members joins the meeting. Guests from other clubs and non-Toastmasters can join in too. Perfect for clubs tallying points for Toastmaster of the Year award!
